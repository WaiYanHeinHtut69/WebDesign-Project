About Us

Founded by passionate manga aficionados Hajime and Naruto, Manga Paradise opened its doors in 2001 with a singular mission: to celebrate and share the artistry, storytelling, and cultural impact of manga. Whether you’re a seasoned collector, a curious newcomer, or simply seeking the latest releases, our shelves are stocked with a diverse array of manga titles, spanning genres from action-packed shonen to heartwarming slice-of-life, from epic fantasy to futuristic sci-fi.
